Our Mission

Our Vision

To become South Africa's most trusted digital information platform, empowering citizens with knowledge and resources.

Our Values

Accuracy, accessibility, user-focus, and continuous improvement guide everything we do.

Why We Created SA Daily Portal

In 2023, we noticed a gap in accessible, consolidated information for everyday South Africans. Government websites were often difficult to navigate, and important information was scattered across multiple sources. We created SA Daily Portal to solve this problem.
