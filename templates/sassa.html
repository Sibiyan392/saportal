Quick Tips for SASSA Applications

Always use your correct ID number and cellphone number
Keep your SASSA reference number after applying
Check your status weekly for updates
Appeal within 30 days if your application is rejected
Update banking details immediately if your bank changes
Use the official SASSA website or WhatsApp line only
