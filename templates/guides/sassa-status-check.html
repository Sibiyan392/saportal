How to Check SASSA Status Online 2026

SA Daily Portal Team February 14, 2026 7 min read

Introduction

If you've applied for the SASSA SRD R350 grant or any other social grant, checking your application status is crucial to know when to expect your payment. SASSA provides several ways to check your grant status, with the online portal being the most convenient method.

Important Note

This guide is updated for 2026 with the latest SASSA procedures. Always verify information on the official SASSA website: www.sassa.gov.za

Method 1: Check Status Online

1 Visit the Official SASSA SRD Website

Go to https://srd.sassa.gov.za. Ensure you're on the official .gov.za domain.

2 Scroll to Status Check Section

Find the "Application Status" or "Check Your Status" section on the homepage.

3 Enter Your ID Number

Enter your 13-digit South African ID number exactly as it appears on your ID document.

4 Enter Your Phone Number

Enter the cellphone number you used when applying for the grant.

5 Complete Security Check

Enter the CAPTCHA characters or answer the simple math question.

6 Click "Check Status"

Your current application status will be displayed on screen.

Understanding Your Status

  • Approved: Your application has been successful
  • Pending: Your application is still being processed
  • Rejected: Your application was not approved
  • Awaiting Banking Details: You need to provide/verify banking information
  • Paid: Payment has been made to your account

Method 2: Check via WhatsApp

1 Save SASSA WhatsApp Number

Save 082 046 8553 in your phone contacts.

2 Send "Hi" or "Hello"

The automated chatbot will respond with a menu of options.

3 Select Status Check Option

Reply with the number corresponding to "Check my SRD grant status".

4 Provide Your ID Number

Send your 13-digit ID number when prompted.

5 Receive Status Update

You'll receive a message with your current application status.

Method 3: Check via USSD

Dial *134*7737# on your cellphone and follow the prompts.

Method 4: Check via Email

Email GrantEnquiries@sassa.gov.za with your ID number and full name. Include "Status Check" in the subject line.

Beware of Scams!

Never pay anyone to check your SASSA status. The official services are FREE. SASSA will never ask for your PIN, password, or bank details via WhatsApp or SMS.

Common Problems and Solutions

"ID Number Not Found"

  • Check that you entered the ID number correctly
  • Ensure you're using the same phone number you applied with
  • Wait at least 7 days after applying before checking status

Website Not Loading

  • Try during off-peak hours (early morning or late evening)
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Use a different browser

SASSA Payment Dates 2026

  • Older Persons Grant: First week of each month
  • Disability Grant: Second week of each month
  • Children's Grants: Third week of each month
  • SRD R350 Grant: From the 25th of each month

Pro Tip: Enable Notifications

After checking your status online, you can opt-in to receive SMS notifications when your payment is processed.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I check my SASSA status?
Checking once a week is sufficient. Status updates typically happen weekly.
Can I check someone else's SASSA status?
Yes, if you have their ID number and the phone number they used for application, and their permission.
What does "Referred to Bank" mean?
SASSA has sent your payment instruction to the bank. It usually takes 1-3 business days to reflect.
My status says "Approved" but I haven't been paid. What should I do?
Wait at least 5 business days after approval. If still not paid, check your banking details are correct.
