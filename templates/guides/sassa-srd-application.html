How to Apply for SASSA SRD R350 Grant 2026

SA Daily Portal Team February 14, 2026 10 min read

Introduction

The SASSA SRD (Social Relief of Distress) R350 grant continues to provide crucial financial assistance to millions of unemployed South Africans in 2026. This grant is designed for individuals who are in dire need of financial support and do not receive any other form of government assistance.

2026 Updates

SASSA has streamlined the application process for 2026. New features include faster verification, improved mobile application options, and better communication about application status.

Who Can Apply? Eligibility Criteria

You ARE eligible if:

  • You are a South African citizen, permanent resident, or refugee
  • You are 18 years or older
  • You are unemployed and not receiving any income
  • You are not receiving any other social grant
  • You are not receiving UIF benefits

You are NOT eligible if:

  • You receive any other SASSA grant
  • You are employed and earning an income
  • You receive UIF payments
  • You are a student receiving NSFAS funding

Required Documents

  • South African ID Number: Your 13-digit identity document number
  • Cellphone Number: An active South African cellphone number registered in your name
  • Banking Details (Optional): Bank name, account number, and branch code if you want payments made directly to your bank account

Step-by-Step Application Guide

Method 1: Online Application

1 Visit the Official SASSA SRD Website

Go to https://srd.sassa.gov.za. Ensure you're on the official .gov.za domain.

2 Click on "Apply Now"

On the homepage, click the "Apply Now" button to start your application.

3 Enter Your ID Number

Enter your 13-digit South African ID number carefully.

4 Enter Your Cellphone Number

Enter the cellphone number you used when applying.

5 Enter the OTP

You'll receive a 6-digit code via SMS. Enter this code to verify your identity.

6 Complete the Application Form

Fill in your personal details, address, and choose your payment method.

7 Review and Submit

Review all information, then click "Submit Application". Save your reference number.

Method 2: WhatsApp Application

  1. Save 082 046 8553 in your phone contacts
  2. Send "Hi" to start the conversation
  3. Follow the prompts to apply for the SRD grant
  4. You'll receive an OTP via SMS to verify your identity

Method 3: USSD Application

Dial *134*7737# and follow the menu instructions.

Important: Cellphone Number

The cellphone number you provide must be active and accessible to you. SASSA will send a one-time PIN (OTP) to this number to verify your identity.

After You Apply

SASSA aims to process applications within 7-14 working days. You can check your status using the same website, WhatsApp, or USSD methods.

Pro Tip: Keep Your Reference Number Safe

Write down your reference number and store it somewhere safe. You'll need it for any future inquiries about your application.

Common Problems and Solutions

"ID Number Already Used"

This means there's already an application associated with your ID. Check your status instead of applying again.

"Cellphone Number Not Registered"

Your cellphone number may not be registered in your name. Visit your mobile network provider to register it.

OTP Not Received

  • Wait at least 5 minutes before trying again
  • Check that you have network signal
  • Try restarting your phone

Scam Alert

SASSA will NEVER ask for your bank PIN, password, or any payment to process your application. If anyone contacts you asking for money to "help" with your application, it's a scam.

Payment Methods

  • Bank Deposit: Money paid directly into your bank account (fastest method)
  • Cash Send: Collect cash at selected retailers like Pick n Pay, Boxer, or Checkers
  • Post Office: Collect cash at your nearest South African Post Office branch

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I apply for the SRD grant if I'm a student?
Yes, students can apply if they are not receiving NSFAS funding or any other form of financial support.
Do I need to reapply every month?
No, you need to reapply every 3 months. SASSA will send you an SMS reminder when it's time to reapply.
Can I change my payment method after applying?
Yes, you can update your payment method by logging into the SRD website and selecting "Update Payment Method".
How will I know if my application is approved?
You will receive an SMS notification when your application status changes. You can also check your status online at any time.
