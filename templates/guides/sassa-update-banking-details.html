How to Update SASSA Banking Details

Step-by-step guide to change your banking details for SASSA SRD and other grants

5 min read Updated 2026
Nompumelelo Sibiya March 2026 SASSA Guide
Security Alert: Never share your banking details with anyone claiming to be from SASSA. Only update via official channels!

Why Update Your Banking Details?

You may need to update your SASSA banking details if:

  • Your bank account is closed or changed
  • You want to switch to a different bank
  • Your account number was entered incorrectly
  • Your payments are showing as "Bank Details Pending"
  • You want to change from cash send to bank transfer

Method 1: Update Online (SRD Grant)

Step-by-Step:
1. Visit srd.sassa.gov.za
2. Click "How to change banking details"
3. Enter your ID number and phone number
4. You'll receive an OTP via SMS
5. Enter the OTP to verify your identity
6. Select "Update Banking Details"
7. Enter your new bank account information
8. Confirm and submit
Required Information:
• Bank name
• Account holder name (must match your ID)
• Account number
• Branch code

Method 2: Update via WhatsApp

WhatsApp Steps:
1. Save 082 046 8553 to your contacts
2. Send "SASSA Banking" via WhatsApp
3. Follow the prompts to verify your identity
4. Enter your new banking details
5. Confirm the information is correct
6. You'll receive a confirmation SMS

Method 3: Update for Other Grants (Child Support, Disability, etc.)

For Permanent Grants:
1. Visit your nearest SASSA office
2. Bring your ID document
3. Bring a bank statement or letter from your bank
4. Fill out the "Change of Banking Details" form
5. Request a receipt as proof of submission

Important Requirements

  • Account must be in your name: The bank account must match the ID number used for the grant
  • Active account: Ensure the account is active and not dormant
  • Bank confirmation letter: May be required for in-person updates
After Updating:
• You will receive an SMS confirmation within 24-48 hours
• Payments will reflect in your new account on the next payment date
• Check your status online to verify the update was successful

Common Banking Details Issues

"Bank Details Pending" Status:
This means SASSA is still verifying your banking information. Wait 3-5 business days. If it persists, contact SASSA.

Payment Failed/Returned:
If your payment fails, SASSA may switch you to cash send. Update your details immediately to resume bank payments.

Fraud Alert!
• SASSA will NEVER ask for your PIN, password, or card details
• Only use official channels: srd.sassa.gov.za or the official WhatsApp number
• Report scams to the SASSA fraud hotline

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does it take for banking details to be updated?
A: Online updates reflect within 24-72 hours. In-person updates may take 3-5 business days.

Q: Can I use a family member's bank account?
A: No, the account must be in your name as it appears on your ID.

Q: What if I don't have a bank account?
A: You can collect your grant at Pick n Pay, Shoprite, Boxer, or USave stores using your ID.
