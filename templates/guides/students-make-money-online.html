How Students Can Make Money Online in South Africa (2026 Guide)

Nompumelelo Sibiya March 2026 11 min read

Student Online Income Facts

  • Average student online earner: R1,500 - R4,500/month
  • Most popular methods: Surveys, freelance writing, tutoring
  • Top students earn: R8,000+ per month
  • Time investment: 10-15 hours per week

1. Online Surveys

OpinionWorld

R20-100 per survey. Pays via EFT or vouchers.

R100 minimum payout

SurveyCompare

R30-80 per survey. Cash via EFT.

SA platform

i-Say

R15-50 per survey. Voucher rewards.

Global brand

2. Micro-Tasks

Clickworker

Data categorization, proofreading, research. Pays via PayPal.

Remotasks

AI training tasks. Training provided.

UserTesting

$10 per 20-min test. Test websites/apps.

3. Freelancing

Fiverr

Create gigs: writing, design, social media. Best for beginners.

Upwork

Bid on projects. Higher rates, more professional.

SchoolAhead

SA tutoring platform. R50-200 per hour.

4. Tutoring

Teach Me 2

R120-250 per hour. All subjects.

Preply

$10-30 per hour. International students.

First-year tutoring

R150-200 per hour. Advertise on campus.

5. Sell Study Notes

Stuvia

Popular in SA. Set your own price.

Oxbridge Notes

50-70% commission. International platform.

6. Transcription

Rev

$0.30-1.10 per minute. Grammar test required.

GoTranscript

$0.60-0.90 per minute. Beginners welcome.

Payment Methods

  • PayPal: Most international platforms. Transfer to SA bank (3-5 days)
  • Payoneer: Lower fees, faster transfers (1-2 days)
  • Direct EFT: SA platforms pay directly to your bank
  • Vouchers: Takealot, Mr Price – useful for essentials

⚠️ Avoid Scams!

  • Never pay for job opportunities
  • "Too good to be true" usually is
  • Never share ID or bank details upfront
  • Stick to platforms listed in this guide

FAQ

I'm a first-year with no skills. Where to start?

Start with surveys and micro-tasks. While doing these, learn a skill (Canva, writing) via free courses. Within 2-3 months, start freelancing.

How many hours should I work?

10-15 hours per week is ideal. This earns R1,500-3,000/month. Prioritize studies first.

Can I earn in dollars?

Yes! Upwork, Fiverr, Rev pay in USD. Use PayPal or Payoneer. Exchange rate works in your favour.

Will earning affect NSFAS?

Generally you can earn up to R2,500/month without affecting funding. Check with your financial aid office for current rules.

7-Day Student Action Plan

  1. Day 1: Sign up for 2-3 survey sites
  2. Day 2: Complete profiles fully
  3. Day 3: Identify subjects for tutoring
  4. Day 4: Create profile on Fiverr/Upwork
  5. Day 5: Take free online course in a skill
  6. Day 6: Apply for transcription platforms
  7. Day 7: Choose one method to focus on
