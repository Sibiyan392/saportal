Privacy Policy for SA Daily Portal

Important Notice: SA Daily Portal is an informational website. We do not collect personal identification information without your consent. This policy explains our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Location Data

When you use our weather service, we may request access to your location to provide accurate weather information. This is optional and requires your explicit permission.

Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies to analyze website traffic, understand user behavior, and improve our services.

Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage statistics including page views, time spent on pages, and browser information to improve user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide accurate weather forecasts and sports information
  • Improvement: To enhance and optimize our website and services
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our content
  • Personalization: To remember your preferences and location settings
  • Advertising: To display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense

3. Google AdSense & Third-Party Services

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings
  • For more information about how Google uses data, visit Google's Privacy & Terms

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information.

  • We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Limited access to data by authorized personnel only

5. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request information about what data we have collected
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of data collection and personalized advertising
  • Cookies: Control cookie settings through your browser
Cookie Control: Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can usually find these settings in the "Options" or "Preferences" menu of your browser.

6. Data Retention

We retain different types of information for different periods:

  • Analytics Data: Up to 26 months
  • Location Data: Only during your current session
  • Cookie Data: As specified in cookie settings (usually 30-365 days)

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
